Portfolio

This portfolio is a complete website built using NodeJS and EJS templating. It contains my numeric business card, my projects, my blog, my resume and a contact section. The contact form uses Nodemailer to send emails to my email without the need to open an email app on the desktop, and the website is hosted on Firebase. The blog uses MongoDB Atlas for hosting.

Secrets

This Secrets App anonymously hosts all the most profound secrets people were willing to share with the help of MongoDB Atlas. It was made possible by using OAuth 2.0. To access the platform, users need to identify themselves via a custom account or by the use of 3rd party apps like Google and Facebook.
